Culture Clash in Conversational AI

What are the effects of conversational AI on its users? How will conversational AI reshape society?

This project investigates how the values and beliefs expressed by conversational AI vary and whether diverging values between humans and AI can have effects similar to conventional culture clash and human adaptation.
